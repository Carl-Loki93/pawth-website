Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: How do I create an account?
A: Download the app and tap "Get Started" to create your profile and add your dog's information.

Q: Is Pawth free to use?
A: Yes! Pawth's core features are completely free. Premium features may be available in the future.

Location & Privacy

Q: Why does Pawth need location access?
A: Location access is required to track your walks, measure distance, and provide AR experiences. You can control location permissions in your device settings.

Q: Is my location data private?
A: Yes! Your location data is encrypted and used only for app functionality. We never sell or share your location with third parties for marketing.

Features & Gameplay

Q: How do I earn bones and XP?
A: Complete daily quests, go on walks, reach distance milestones, and participate in community activities to earn rewards.

Q: Can I use Pawth without AR features?
A: Absolutely! While AR adds fun experiences, all core features work without camera access.

Technical Issues

Q: The app isn't tracking my walk correctly.
A: Ensure location permissions are enabled and you have a good GPS signal. Try restarting the app if issues persist.

Q: I'm not receiving notifications.
A: Check that notifications are enabled in both the app settings and your device settings for Pawth.
